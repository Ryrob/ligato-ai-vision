What It Handles

Every way a customer can show you the problem.

From a late-night photo to a live screen-share, the AI Vision Assistant turns messy customer input into a clean, actionable job.

Voice-first conversation

Alex, your existing AI receptionist, runs the whole call. No new app, no chat thread to babysit — just the call and the voice the customer already expects.

MMS as the visual channel

MMS is strictly for sending photos and short videos. Customers get a simple "Got it — reviewing now" confirmation. No typing long messages. The voice agent handles the rest.

Live Video Calls

When pictures aren't enough, the AI hops on a live call, watches the camera or the shared screen, and narrates what it's seeing in real time.

Emergency Triage

Active leaks, gas smells, sparking — flagged instantly and escalated to you, not sitting in a queue.

Pre-Visit Prep Work

The AI walks the customer through shutting off water, flipping breakers, or clearing access — before the tech arrives.

Tech Handoff

Your plumber gets a one-page brief: stills, diagnosis, parts likely needed, and a shut-off status. Less guesswork, fewer second trips.

How It Works

From photo to booked job in under two minutes.

No app to download. No account to create. Just text the number — or tap the "Video Call" button on your site.
